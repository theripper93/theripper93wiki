# Party HUD

<ModuleInfo/>

A system agnostic HUD that shows party members and their summons/companions in a neat and tidy manner. It also shows configurable stats and conditions for each member.

## How to use

The module requires [Carousel Combat Tracker](../free/carousel-combat-tracker) to be installed and enabled.
All the information for tooltips and resources is pulled and mirrored from the combat tracker.

- Hover over the HUD to show the buttons.
- Toggle the HUD on/off with the button on top of the HUD.
- Toggle the Scene Controls with the button on top of the HUD.
